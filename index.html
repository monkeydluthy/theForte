What is ESI?

My name is Dustin Wolf, some may know me as RAT or Dustin Froehlich before my name change. Iâ€™m 34 years old, trauma survivor of losing both parents young, homelessness and much more and skateboarding saved my life. It gave me all the needed skills and characteristics to build me up. To the point of joining the military and finding my version of success in life. Skateboarding taught me ,commitment, resilience, independence ,confidence and much more i now take these and charge at my mission full force to give back to what gave so much to me and my passed loved one Zack Forte.

The proposed non-profit indoor/outdoor extreme sport institute ( named ESI , with internal training and mentorship The Forte) aims to become the premier destination for sports enthusiasts in Bristol, Connecticut, leveraging the city's status as the home of ESPN and its strong sporting culture. The facility will cater to various extreme sports, such as skateboarding, BMX biking, rock climbing, trampoline sports, ,parkor possibly thinking about ninja warrior course also! The goal is to create a safe, inclusive, and inspiring environment for athletes of all ages and skill levels while promoting health, wellness, and community engagement. Dedicated to local art, culture and community we will change and positively impact thousands of lives."
